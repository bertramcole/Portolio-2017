Identity System | Branding

An Identity Systems is given to corporations and people to differentiate them from the rest. As Humans, we all have been defined by our actions and by how the world perceives those actions. Being accountable for your actions is easy, yet it important that a business also addresses the way that it is going to be perceived. That when you need to build a visual identity that lets your public image show off your best attributes and what true convictions you stand behind. Because if no one knows what you are or what you stand for then how can they fallow you, you have give no direction.

People fallow CONVICTIONS.

Layout | Graphic Logo's

Identity System Design being my focus, I have developed a talent for seeing big picture concepts. Most would say that it all visually starts with a logo, yet that couldn't be more incorrect, a good identity starts with good reason. I make my design decisions off reason and purpose. Therefore the theme and layout and implementation of these are the foundation of what this logo and in the end this identity system will depend on, it will be how much reason there is for the cohesive and individuality of this Brand that make people believe.

People believe REASON.

Web Design

A Website is the contemporary medium of this era, with the evolution of the internet in the last 20 years radically changing a lot of design practices. The shift has even brought forth a new area of design UX/UI with interaction and experience gaining a greater focus. The web to an Identity System or brand is their presence, their physical space. People can see an advertisement and remember the product or brand yet it going to take them going and looking it up online. Your website should be ready to deliver all the answers and physical experience of your brand. Interaction with a web page is a physical experience and people must feel the value of professionalism.

People value EXPERIENCE.

Social Media Marketing

Social Media's like Facebook and Twitter, have brought forth a new ear of Analytical Marketing, with the growth of user data and the acceptability to more and more people. It no longer takes millions of dollars and many man hours to have a global marketing campaign. Yet now what is making the difference is the way we use these channels like Facebook and Twitter. That being smart with user data to better target your audience is a must. Growing up with social media, my first myspace being when I was 12 and being on every other social platform as they come and go has really left me with this universal understanding of what the point of all these social media's sites and their popularity. The point is to keep the User entertained. Social media allows constant updating and engaging of your audience.

People need ENTERTAINMENT.
